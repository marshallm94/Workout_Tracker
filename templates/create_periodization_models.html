Create Periodization Model

Create periodization models. A single periodization model can be
applied to more than one exercise in a mesocycle. Note that the
length of your input will determine the number of microcycles in
this mesocycle. For example, if you were to input:

Sets Reps RPE
3 12 7
3 10 8
3 8 9
3 6 10

Your mesocycle would be 4 microcycles long, and would be:

Microcycle 1 3 x 12 @ 7
Microcycle 2 3 x 10 @ 8
Microcycle 3 3 x 8 @ 9
Microcycle 3 3 x 6 @ 10
