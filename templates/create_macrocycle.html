Create a macrocycle (aka "program")

Different exercises within a microcycle (aka "split") might follow
different periodization models over the course of a macrocycle (or
mesocycle). The approach this application takes is to create the
microcycle/split first, and then go through that microcycle and
apply a periodization strategy to each movement.


Note that this application does NOT keep track of off days
(i.e. if your microcycle lasts one calendar week but you only
train 4 days out of that week, the split you create should be a 4
day microcycle). With that in mind, how many workouts will there
be in your microcycle?
