Create a Mesocycle

Different exercises in a split will likely follow different
periodization models over the course of a mesocycle.
The approach this application takes is to create the
microcycle/split first, without assigning
sets/reps/RPE/RIR to any movement, and then go through
that microcycle and apply a periodization strategy to each
movement individually.


Note that this application does NOT keep track of off days
(i.e. if your microcycle lasts one calendar week but you only
train 4 days out of that week, the split you create should be a 4
day microcycle). With that in mind, how many workouts will there
be in your microcycle?
